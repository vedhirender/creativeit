Who we are and what we do?

Creative IT is an information technology solutions company providing repair and network solution support to homes, businesses and schools throughout Ireland. We offer a comprehensive range of services.

Established in 2009, Creative IT initially served the Mullingar area where we experienced immediate success which led to the expansion of our services not only in the greater Mullingar area, but also in the midlands region of Ireland and surrounding counties. Our mission is to provide fast and quality based solutions to our clients’ computer service requirements at cost effective prices. We maintain a staff of highly qualified IT systems and computer repair technicians who have been factory trained on all industry products. All our computer repair technicians are ready to answer all queries in a friendly and patient manner and are designated to provide “on-site” or remote access support to ensure a prompt resolution to any IT related issue.

A customer orientated approach is integrated into all services in order to maintain our high levels of customer satisfaction. Fast turnaround is promised with all computer repair solutions as we are aware that the impact associated with any computer malfunction can cause serious disruption to business and home environments. We guarantee all our work with a 6 month warranty and a free collect & return service is offered to residents from the Mullingar area while our staff will organise a swift courier if you reside outside this region.
